    • ○ 125 gives 3 5‘s once

Key property of unit digits

  • ● If you sum two numbers n1 and n2 together, the unit digit of the sum is the unit digit of the sum of units digits of n1 and unit digits of n2
  • ● If you multiply two numbers n1 and n2 together, the unit digit of the product is the unit digit of the product of units digits of n1 and unit digits of n2
  • ● What is 6437 . 7654 ?
  • A) 49268796 B) 49268797 or C) 49268798
  • ● The answer is C as 7.4 = 28

Mersenne Primes and Perfection

  • ● Note a Mersenne prime is a prime number that is one less than a power of two.
  • ● If 2k-1 is a prime number (a Mersenne prime), then MP = 2k-1(2k-1) is a perfect number and every even perfect number has this form.
  • ● Sum of factors of MP is
    • ○ SF(MP) = (1 + 2 + 22 + .. + 2k-1) (1 + (2k-1)) if (2k-1) is prime
    • ○ The first sum is just (2k-1) and so SF(MP) = (2k-1)2k = 2 MP is perfect
  • ● Suppose n = 2k-1(2k-1) where(2k-1) is NOT prime but say (2k-1) = p1p2 where and p1and p2 are primes. 2 k-1
    • ○ Then SF(n) = (1 + 2 + 2 + .. + 2 )(1 + p1+ p2 + p1p2 )
    • ○ = (2k-1)(2k+ p1+ p2 ) which is > 2n proving we need Mersenne primes
